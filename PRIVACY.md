# Zterm Privacy Policy

Zterm is an open-source terminal application. It does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

## What Zterm does NOT do

- No telemetry or usage analytics are sent anywhere
- No account creation or login required
- No data is sent to any remote server by default
- No crash reports are submitted automatically
- No AI conversation history leaves your machine (when using local models)

## Local storage

Zterm stores configuration, themes, and session data locally on your machine only, in your OS application data directory.

## Third-party services

If you configure Zterm to use a cloud AI provider (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic), your prompts are sent directly to that provider under their own privacy policy. Zterm itself does not proxy or log those requests.

## Questions or concerns

Please open an issue at https://github.com/tzero86/Zterm/issues
